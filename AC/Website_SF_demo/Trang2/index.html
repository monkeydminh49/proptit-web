About us 2014

Systematic Functions, formerly mFunctions, was established in 2016 to develop and implement the mStore platform, a digital Wholesaling and Micro-retailing platform for exporting FMCG products from Vietnam and creating a disruptive DigiPhysical® distribution channel in Myanmar.

As a trusted strategic partner to major corporations in Vietnam and Myanmar, Systematic Functions focuses on developing cutting-edge technology platforms in sectors like retailing, telecommunications, big data, and logistics. We have a team of experienced professionals with expertise in digital technology and business model development. We offer comprehensive business and technology advisory and implementation services.
